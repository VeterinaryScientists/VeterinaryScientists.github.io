The national network of Veterinary Scientists in training

We're a student led organization representing combined DVM-PhD programs across the United States.

Our Mission

We aim to promote the education of veterinary researchers, and to build a community of researchers who value and understand the relationship between people and animals. It has long been understood that the bond between humans and animals is a fundamental part of our daily lives, and our goal is to use our understanding of veterinary medicine to explore this relationship further to benefit all species.

What we do

Improving veterinary research

The NAVS is a community of DVM-PhD students striving to improve animal and human health.

Career exploration

There is no one "typical" career path for a DVM-PhD. Our graduates have diverse careers in academia, government, biotech, and other industries. Our organization builds connections between current students and alumni so that our members can learn about new career opportunities.

Creating community

NAVS continuously seeks to improve communications among combined DVM-PhD program through connecting in persion at our annual meeting, via our executive board, which consists of one student representative from each school.

Learning together

Every year since 2017 students from combined DVM-PhD programs around the country have attended an annual colloquium to share our research, build our community, and explore career opportunities for veterinary scientists .
