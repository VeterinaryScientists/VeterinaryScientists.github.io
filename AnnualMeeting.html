Virtual NAVS colloquium

August 3-4 2021

The 2021 NAVS Colloquium will take place in virtually 1-5pm EDT on August 3rd and 4th. Our colloquium is paired with the National Veterinary Scholars Symposium. More info on NVSS can be found here: "http://veterinaryscholars.boehringer-ingelheim.com/national-symposium

Registration

Registration for the colloquium and NVSS closes June 9th . Please register using the google form emailed to all current NAVS members. Due to our affiliation with NVSS and our use of their iPoster platform, we are not offering colloquium-only registration this year, but all fees will be covered by NAVS. If you have any questions about registration, please contact our communications leads at NAVS.communications@gmail.com.

Abstract submission

Current DVM/VMD PhD students are invited to submit an abstract for the 2021 Colloquium using the google form emailed to all current NAVS members. The abstract submission deadline is June 21st . We are excited to announce that we will be offering live, 10-minute oral presentations to six students based on reviewer scores. All other abstracts will be accepted for poster presentations. Please follow the submission guidelines described on the form. Abstracts failing to adhere to the instructions will be rejected.

Call for NVSS Moderators

NVSS is recruiting ~50 moderators for their poster sessions (1 moderator/session). They are interested in recruiting combined degree students to moderate, and you can opt to moderate sessions with topics related to your interests. If you would like to assist, please let us know by June 30th by filling out the appropriate google form emailed to all current NAVS members. Note: this is not an interest form; filling it out indicates commitment to moderate.
