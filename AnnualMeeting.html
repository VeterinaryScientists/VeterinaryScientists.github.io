Virtual NAVS colloquium

August 3-4 2021

The 2021 NAVS Colloquium will take place in virtually on August 3rd and 4th. Look out for a call for abstracts for current combined DVM-PhD students in the beginning of June. Our colloquium is paired with the National Veterinary Scholars Symposium. More info on NVSS can be found here: "http://veterinaryscholars.boehringer-ingelheim.com/national-symposium
